Will you arrange for a driver's license for me?

yes, We start processing your license right from the day we present you to DVLA for your learner's license.

Is it compulsory that I go to DVLA for the tests?

Yes. it is only DVLA that can issue a valid driing license for student drivers. Therefore, the theory and practical tests are compulsory.

Can I choose a preferred transmission (Manual or Automatically)?

For the regular and express packages, we take you through both manual and automatic transmission. However, if a trainee insists on one, we stick to their selected transmission. For the polishing, we only train students on the transmission they are already familiar with.

Do you have discounts?

Yes, we do.There are discounts for students and national services.

Can I choose my preferred instructor?

Unfortunately no. instructors are assigned to practical session based on their availability.

I can drive. Can I get a drivers license only?

Yes, you can.
